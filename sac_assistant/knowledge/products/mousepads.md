## Frequently Asked Questions — Mousepads
- Q: Which mousepad is better for low-DPI, high-precision aiming?
  A: The Dazz Speed's low-friction surface favors fast mouse movement, which generally suits
  higher-DPI play styles. For low-DPI, high-precision aiming, a "control"-oriented surface
  like the Corsair MM300 Extended's balanced cloth typically feels more predictable.
- Q: Can the Corsair MM300 Extended fit under a mechanical keyboard with feet extended?
  A: Yes, its cloth surface is thin enough to sit flat under most mechanical keyboards, even
  with their rear feet extended for a typing angle.
