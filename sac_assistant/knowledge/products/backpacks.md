# Backpacks Catalog

## Laptop Backpack [Razer Rogue Backpack V3 15"]
Designed for professionals and gamers, the Rogue Backpack V3 provides excellent protection, durability, and organization for daily commuting and travel.

- Material: Water-resistant recycled polyester
- Compatible laptop sizes: Up to 15.6 inches
- Capacity: 24 liters
- Water resistant: Yes (splash and light rain, not submersion)
- Padded compartments: Laptop sleeve, tablet sleeve, organizer pocket
- Available colors: Black
- Price: USD $99.99

## Laptop Backpack [Corsair Carbide Backpack]
A rugged backpack built for transporting a gaming laptop and peripherals, with reinforced corners and a dedicated cable organization pocket.

- Material: Ballistic nylon
- Compatible laptop sizes: Up to 17 inches
- Capacity: 28 liters
- Water resistant: Yes (splash resistant, not submersion)
- Padded compartments: Laptop sleeve, peripheral pocket, cable organizer
- Available colors: Black, Gray
- Price: USD $79.99

## Compatibility — Backpacks
- Both backpacks fit most 13"–15.6" laptops in dedicated padded sleeves; the Corsair Carbide
  Backpack additionally fits laptops up to 17 inches.
- Neither backpack is fully waterproof; both are described as water-resistant, which means
  they tolerate light rain and splashes but should not be submerged or left in heavy rain
  for extended periods.

## Frequently Asked Questions — Backpacks
- Q: Can these backpacks fit a 17-inch gaming laptop?
  A: Only the Corsair Carbide Backpack fits laptops up to 17 inches. The Razer Rogue
  Backpack V3 is rated for laptops up to 15.6 inches and may not comfortably fit larger
  17-inch models.
- Q: Are these backpacks fully waterproof?
  A: No. Both are water-resistant, meaning they handle light rain and splashes, but they are
  not designed for submersion or prolonged exposure to heavy rain.
- Q: Is there room for a mouse and keyboard in addition to the laptop?
  A: Yes, both have separate organizer compartments suitable for a mouse, cables, and small
  accessories, in addition to the dedicated laptop sleeve.
