# Guarantee and Returns Policy

## Warranty Period

All products are covered by the manufacturer's warranty starting from the date of delivery.

- Standard warranty: 2 years from the purchase date
- Extended warranty (if applicable): Available for selected products

## What the Warranty Covers

The warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use.

- Covered:
  - Manufacturing defects
  - Faulty components
  - Hardware malfunctions under normal operating conditions

- Not covered:
  - Accidental damage
  - Liquid damage
  - Improper use or misuse
  - Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  - Cosmetic damage

## Return and Exchange Policy

Customers may request a return or exchange within the return period.

- Return window: 30 days from delivery
- Conditions:
  - Product must be in its original condition
  - Original packaging and accessories must be included
  - Proof of purchase is required

## How to Request a Refund or Exchange

- Step 1: Contact customer support and provide your order number.
- Step 2: Explain the issue and submit the request.
- Step 3: Follow the return instructions provided by the support team.
- Step 4: After inspection, the refund or exchange will be approved.

- Refund timeline: 5–10 business days after approval
