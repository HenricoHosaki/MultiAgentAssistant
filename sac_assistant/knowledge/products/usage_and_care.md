# Usage and Care

## Cleaning and Maintenance

To ensure the longevity and performance of your products, follow the recommended care instructions below.

- Recommended cleaning method:
  - Use a soft microfiber cloth.
  - For keyboards, use compressed air to remove dust between keys.
  - For headsets, gently wipe ear cushions and surfaces with a slightly damp cloth.
  - For backpacks, clean with a damp cloth and mild soap when necessary.

- What to avoid:
  - Harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners
  - Submerging products in water
  - Excessive moisture
  - Direct exposure to heat sources
  - Sharp objects that may scratch surfaces

## Water and Dust Resistance

Some products provide limited protection against environmental conditions.

- Which products:
  - Razer Rogue Backpack V3: Water-resistant exterior
  - Razer BlackShark V2 Pro: Limited resistance to sweat during regular use

- What "water-resistant" means here:
  - Protection against light splashes and moisture
  - Not waterproof
  - Not designed for immersion in water or heavy rain exposure

## Storage Recommendations

Store products properly when not in use to maximize their lifespan.

- Recommended conditions:
  - Temperature between 10°C and 35°C
  - Dry and well-ventilated environment
  - Away from direct sunlight
  - Away from excessive humidity and dust
  - Keep cables loosely coiled to prevent damage

## Common Usage Questions

**Can I use the keyboard with a tablet?**

Yes, the keyboard can be used with tablets that support USB peripherals, either directly or through a compatible USB adapter.

**Does the headset work with consoles?**

Yes, the headset is compatible with most modern consoles, including PlayStation and Xbox, depending on the connection method and model compatibility.

**Can I customize the keyboard lighting?**

Yes, RGB lighting can be customized using Razer Synapse software.

**Can the backpack be used for travel?**

Yes, the backpack is designed for daily commuting and travel, offering dedicated compartments for laptops and accessories.
