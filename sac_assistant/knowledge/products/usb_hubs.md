# USB Hubs and Docks Catalog

## USB-C Hub [Anker 7-in-1 PowerExpand]
A compact USB-C hub that adds HDMI, USB-A, and SD card support to laptops with limited ports.

- Ports: 1x HDMI (4K@30Hz), 3x USB-A 3.0, 1x USB-C (data), 1x SD card reader, 1x USB-C (power delivery passthrough)
- Power delivery: Up to 100W passthrough charging
- Material: Aluminum housing
- Cable length: 15 cm (integrated)
- Compatible platforms: Windows, macOS, ChromeOS
- Price: USD $39.99

## USB-C Docking Station [CalDigit TS3 Plus]
A desktop docking station for power users, offering extensive port expansion and high power delivery for ultrabooks and laptops.

- Ports: 2x Thunderbolt 3, 5x USB-A 3.0, 1x USB-C (data), 1x Gigabit Ethernet, 1x SD card reader, 1x audio in/out
- Power delivery: Up to 87W passthrough charging
- Material: Aluminum housing
- Cable length: 0.5 m (integrated Thunderbolt 3 cable)
- Compatible platforms: Windows, macOS (Thunderbolt 3 required for full functionality)
- Price: USD $239.99

## Compatibility — USB Hubs and Docks
- The Anker 7-in-1 PowerExpand works over a standard USB-C port and does not require
  Thunderbolt; it is compatible with most modern laptops, though 4K output is limited to
  30Hz due to USB-C video bandwidth (not Thunderbolt).
- The CalDigit TS3 Plus requires a Thunderbolt 3 (or compatible Thunderbolt 4) port to unlock
  its full bandwidth, dual-display support, and maximum power delivery; connecting it to a
  standard USB-C port without Thunderbolt will significantly limit its functionality.

## Frequently Asked Questions — USB Hubs and Docks
- Q: Can I charge my laptop while using the hub/dock?
  A: Yes, both support power delivery passthrough — up to 100W on the Anker hub and up to
  87W on the CalDigit dock — though actual charging speed depends on your laptop's own
  charging capabilities.
- Q: Do I need a Thunderbolt port for the Anker 7-in-1 PowerExpand?
  A: No, it works over standard USB-C. The CalDigit TS3 Plus, however, does require
  Thunderbolt 3 (or Thunderbolt 4 with backward compatibility) for full performance.
- Q: Can I connect two external monitors?
  A: Only the CalDigit TS3 Plus supports dual external displays (via its two Thunderbolt 3
  ports), and only on laptops with a Thunderbolt 3/4 port. The Anker 7-in-1 PowerExpand
  supports a single external display via its HDMI port.
