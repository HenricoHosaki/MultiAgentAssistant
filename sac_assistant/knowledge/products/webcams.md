# Webcams Catalog

- Field of view: 78 degrees
- Autofocus: Yes

## Streaming Webcam [Razer Kiyo]

- Resolution: 1080p at 30fps / 720p at 60fps
- Field of view: 81.6 degrees
- Autofocus: Yes

- Connectivity: Wired (USB)

- Tripod compatible: Yes
- Available colors: Black
- Price: USD $99.99

## Compatibility — Webcams
- Both webcams are plug-and-play UVC (USB Video Class) devices, compatible with Windows,
  macOS, and most Linux distributions without additional drivers.
- Both work with common streaming and conferencing software (OBS, Zoom, Microsoft Teams,
  Google Meet) out of the box.
- Logitech's companion software (Logi Tune) is optional and only needed for advanced image
  adjustments on the C920.

## Frequently Asked Questions — Webcams
- Q: Which one is better for a dark room?
  A: The Razer Kiyo, because of its built-in adjustable ring light, performs noticeably
  better in low-light conditions than the C920, which relies only on software-based exposure
  correction.
- Q: Can I use the microphone instead of a separate headset mic?
  A: The C920's stereo microphones are good enough for casual calls, but for streaming or
  professional audio, a dedicated microphone is recommended over either webcam's built-in mic.
- Q: Do these support 4K recording?
  A: No, both are limited to 1080p maximum resolution. Neither model supports 4K capture.
