# Gaming Chairs Catalog

## Gaming Chair [DXRacer Formula Series]
An ergonomic gaming chair with a racing-inspired design, adjustable armrests, and a reclining backrest for long gaming or work sessions.

- Material: PU leather upholstery, steel frame
- Weight capacity: Up to 100 kg
- Recline range: 90° to 135°
- Armrests: 2D adjustable (height and angle)
- Lumbar support: Removable lumbar cushion included
- Headrest: Removable headrest pillow included
- Wheelbase: 5-point steel base, PU casters
- Available colors: Black/Red, Black/Blue
- Price: USD $329.99

## Gaming Chair [ThunderX3 EC3]
A breathable mesh-back gaming chair designed for better airflow during long sessions, with a more relaxed, ergonomic profile than racing-style chairs.

- Material: Mesh backrest, fabric seat, steel frame
- Weight capacity: Up to 130 kg
- Recline range: 90° to 165°
- Armrests: 3D adjustable (height, angle, and depth)
- Lumbar support: Built-in adjustable lumbar support
- Headrest: Built-in, non-removable
- Wheelbase: 5-point steel base, rubber casters
- Available colors: Black/Gray
- Price: USD $249.99

## Compatibility — Gaming Chairs
- Both chairs use a standard 5-point base compatible with most class-4 gas lift cylinders for
  future replacement parts.
- Assembly is required for both and typically takes 20–30 minutes with the included tools.
- Neither chair is electrically adjustable; all height and recline adjustments are manual.

## Frequently Asked Questions — Gaming Chairs
- Q: Which chair is better for hot climates or long summer sessions?
  A: The ThunderX3 EC3, because its mesh backrest allows more airflow than the PU leather
  upholstery of the DXRacer Formula Series, which tends to retain more heat.
- Q: Can a taller person (over 1.90m) use these chairs comfortably?
  A: The ThunderX3 EC3's higher weight capacity and deeper recline range generally suit
  taller or heavier users better than the DXRacer Formula Series, though both have fixed
  maximum dimensions and may feel tight for very tall users.
- Q: Do these chairs come pre-assembled?
  A: No, both ship partially disassembled and require manual assembly using the tools and
  instructions included in the box.
