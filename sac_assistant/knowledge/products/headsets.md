# Headsets Catalog

## Wireless Headset [Razer BlackShark V2 Pro]
A premium wireless esports headset featuring exceptional microphone quality, immersive sound, and long battery life for gaming and professional communication.

- Material: Steel-reinforced frame, memory foam ear cushions, synthetic leather
- Driver size: 50 mm Razer TriForce drivers
- Surround sound: THX Spatial Audio
- Microphone: Detachable, HyperClear cardioid
- Battery life: Up to 70 hours
- Noise cancelling: Yes (passive noise isolation)
- Connectivity: Wireless (2.4 GHz)
- Available colors: Black, White
- Price: USD $199.99

## Gaming Headset [HyperX Cloud II]
A comfortable over-ear gaming headset with virtual 7.1 surround sound and a detachable noise-cancelling microphone.

- Material: Memory foam ear cushions, aluminum frame
- Driver size: 53 mm drivers
- Surround sound: Virtual 7.1 (USB audio control box)
- Microphone: Detachable, noise-cancelling
- Battery life: Not applicable (wired)
- Noise cancelling: Yes (microphone only)
- Connectivity: Wired (3.5mm + USB)
- Available colors: Black/Red, Black/Gunmetal
- Price: USD $99.99

## Gaming Headset [Corsair HS65 Surround]
A lightweight wired headset with a durable memory foam headband and Dolby Audio surround sound support via USB.

- Material: Aluminum frame, memory foam ear cushions
- Driver size: 50 mm drivers
- Surround sound: Dolby Audio 7.1 (via USB DAC)
- Microphone: Detachable, omnidirectional
- Battery life: Not applicable (wired)
- Noise cancelling: No (microphone is omnidirectional, not noise-cancelling)
- Connectivity: Wired (3.5mm + USB)
- Available colors: Black, Blue
- Price: USD $69.99

## Compatibility — Headsets
- All three headsets work with PC (Windows/macOS) via their wired or wireless connection.
- The BlackShark V2 Pro's wireless dongle is PC-only; it does not pair via Bluetooth to phones.
- The HyperX Cloud II and Corsair HS65 work on PlayStation and Xbox consoles via the 3.5mm
  connection, but surround sound (virtual 7.1 / Dolby Audio) only works through the USB
  connection on PC.

## Frequently Asked Questions — Headsets
- Q: Does the BlackShark V2 Pro work on PlayStation 5?
  A: It works via the 3.5mm cable included in the box for basic audio, but the wireless
  2.4 GHz dongle and THX Spatial Audio are PC-only features.
- Q: Which of these has the best microphone for streaming?
  A: The BlackShark V2 Pro's HyperClear cardioid microphone is the most directional and
  rejects background noise the best, making it the strongest pick for streaming or
  professional calls among the three.
- Q: Can I replace the ear cushions?
  A: Replacement ear cushions are officially sold for the BlackShark V2 Pro and HyperX Cloud
  II. The Corsair HS65 does not have officially sold replacement cushions at this time.
