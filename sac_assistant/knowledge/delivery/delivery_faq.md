# Delivery FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions — Delivery
- Q: Can I change the delivery address after placing the order?
  A: Only before the order is dispatched. Once a tracking code is issued, the address is
  locked for that shipment.
- Q: What happens if I miss all delivery attempts?
  A: After 3 failed attempts, the package is held at the local distribution center for 7
  days, then returned to our warehouse if not picked up.
- Q: Do you ship to PO boxes?
  A: No. We ship to residential and business addresses, including apartment lockers or
  concierge services for standard-sized packages.
- Q: My package arrived damaged. What do I do?
  A: Take photos of the damage and packaging within 48 hours and contact support with your
  order number. Approved claims get a free replacement or refund without the standard return
  process.
- Q: Why did I receive two tracking codes for one order?
  A: Orders with items from different warehouses may ship as separate packages, each with
  its own tracking code, possibly dispatched at different times.
- Q: Is express/expedited shipping available?
  A: Express shipping is not currently offered. All orders follow the standard timeframes:
  5–10 business days domestic, 15–30 business days international.
- Q: Who pays for re-shipping if I entered the wrong address?
  A: The customer covers the re-shipping cost if the address error was provided by the
  customer. If the error originated from our system, re-shipping is free.
