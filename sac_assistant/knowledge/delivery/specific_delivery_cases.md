# Specific Delivery Cases

## Failed Delivery Attempts
The carrier makes up to 3 delivery attempts at the registered address. If all 3 attempts
fail (no one available to receive the package), the order is held at the local distribution
center for 7 calendar days. If not picked up within that window, it is returned to our
warehouse and the customer is contacted to arrange a new delivery (a new shipping fee may
apply) or a refund.

## Wrong or Incomplete Address
If a package is returned due to an incorrect or incomplete address provided by the
customer, the customer is responsible for the cost of re-shipping once the correct address
is confirmed. If the address error originated from our system (e.g., a data entry mistake
during order processing), re-shipping is free of charge.

## Split Shipments
Orders with items from different warehouses or with different stock availability may be
shipped in multiple packages, each with its own tracking code. All tracking codes are sent
to the customer's email as each package is dispatched, not necessarily at the same time.

## Address Change After Order Placement
An address change request is only possible before the order is dispatched. Once a tracking
code has been issued, the address can no longer be changed; the customer must wait for the
delivery attempt or contact support after a failed attempt to arrange redirection through
the carrier, where available.

## Delivery to PO Boxes and Lockers
We do not ship to PO boxes. Delivery to apartment building lockers or concierge services is
accepted, provided the locker/concierge can receive packages of standard size; oversized
items (such as monitors or gaming chairs) may require a person to be present to receive them
directly.

## Damaged Package on Arrival
If a package arrives visibly damaged, the customer should refuse delivery when possible, or
if already received, take photos of the damage and the packaging within 48 hours and contact
support with the order number. Damaged-in-transit claims approved within this window are
eligible for a free replacement or full refund, without needing to follow the standard
return process.
