# Shipping and Delivery Policy

## Shipping Coverage

We ship to addresses nationwide and internationally.

- Domestic shipping: available to all regions
- International shipping: available to most countries, subject to customs regulations of the destination country

## Delivery Timeframes

- Domestic orders: 5–10 business days from order confirmation
- International orders: 15–30 business days from order confirmation, depending on destination and customs clearance
- Delays caused by customs inspection are outside our control and not covered by delivery guarantees

## Free Shipping

- Orders of R$25 or more qualify for free shipping
- Orders below R$25 are charged a shipping fee calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight

## Order Tracking

- Every order receives a tracking code once it is dispatched
- The tracking code is sent by email and is also available in the customer's order history
- Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to appear after dispatch

## Lost or Delayed Packages

- If a package exceeds the estimated delivery window, contact customer support with the order number for investigation
- Packages confirmed as lost in transit are eligible for a replacement or refund
