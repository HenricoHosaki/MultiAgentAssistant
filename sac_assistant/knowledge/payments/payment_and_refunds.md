# Payment and Refund Policy

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit card
- Debit card
- Pix
- Boleto (bank slip)

## Installments

- Credit card purchases can be split into installments, subject to the number of installments offered at checkout
- Installment availability and limits may vary by card issuer

## Refund Timelines by Payment Method

- Pix: refunded within up to 3 calendar days
- Credit card: refunded within 1 to 2 billing cycles, depending on the card issuer
- Boleto: refunded within 3 business days
- Debit card: refunded within 5 to 30 days, depending on the issuing bank and the merchant's policy

## How Refunds Are Processed

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method used in the purchase
- Refund processing begins only after the return or cancellation is approved
- Processing times above are estimates set by the payment provider or bank, not by us
