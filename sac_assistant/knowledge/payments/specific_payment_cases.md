# Specific Payment Cases

## Duplicate Charges
If a customer is charged more than once for the same order due to a processing error, the
duplicate charge is automatically identified and refunded within 5 business days. If it is
not refunded automatically within that window, the customer should contact support with
both transaction IDs for manual review.

## Failed Payment Retry Policy
If a credit or debit card payment fails (e.g., insufficient funds, card declined), the
order is held in "pending payment" status for 24 hours, during which the customer can retry
with the same or a different payment method. After 24 hours without a successful payment,
the order is automatically cancelled and the reserved stock is released.

## Installment Cancellation Mid-Plan
If an order paid in installments is cancelled or returned after some installments have
already been charged, the remaining installments are cancelled going forward, and the
installments already paid are refunded according to the standard refund timeline for credit
cards (1 to 2 billing cycles).

## International Card Surcharges
Payments made with cards issued outside the customer's shipping country may incur an
international transaction fee charged by the card issuer or bank, which is not collected or
controlled by us. The exact fee, if any, depends on the cardholder's bank.

## Partial Refunds for Partial Cancellations
If a customer cancels only part of a multi-item order, the refund is calculated
proportionally to the cancelled item(s) only, using the same payment method and timeline as
a full refund for that payment type.

## Chargebacks and Payment Disputes
If a customer opens a chargeback or dispute directly with their bank or card issuer instead
of contacting our support first, the order is placed on hold pending the outcome of the
dispute investigation, which is managed by the card issuer and can take 30 to 90 days. We
recommend contacting support directly first, as most issues are resolved faster through our
standard refund process.
