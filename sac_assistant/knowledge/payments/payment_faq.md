# Payment FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions — Payments
- Q: I was charged twice for the same order. What do I do?
  A: Duplicate charges are usually refunded automatically within 5 business days. If not,
  contact support with both transaction IDs for manual review.
- Q: My card payment failed. Will my order be cancelled immediately?
  A: No, the order stays in "pending payment" for 24 hours so you can retry. After 24 hours
  without a successful payment, it is automatically cancelled.
- Q: What happens to my remaining installments if I return the product?
  A: Future installments are cancelled, and the installments already charged are refunded
  following the standard credit card refund timeline (1 to 2 billing cycles).
- Q: Will I be charged extra for using a foreign credit card?
  A: Possibly, but that fee is set and charged by your card issuer or bank, not by us — we
  don't control or collect international transaction fees.
- Q: Can I cancel just one item from a multi-item order?
  A: Yes, and the refund is calculated proportionally for the cancelled item only, using the
  same payment method and refund timeline as a full cancellation.
- Q: Should I dispute a charge with my bank instead of contacting support?
  A: We recommend contacting support first. Bank/card disputes place the order on hold for
  30–90 days while the issuer investigates, which is usually slower than our direct refund
  process.
- Q: Do you support recurring/subscription payments?
  A: No, all purchases are one-time payments. We do not offer subscription or recurring
  billing at this time.
